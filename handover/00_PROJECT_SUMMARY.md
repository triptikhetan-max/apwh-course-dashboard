# AP World History — Project Summary

## The Problem

AP World History students at Alpha Schools are failing because of a writing and source analysis gap, not a content gap. The exam is 60% writing (DBQ 25% + SAQ 20% + LEQ 15%) and 40% MCQ. All four at-risk students scored 1-2 out of 7 on the DBQ with the same four failures: no thesis, vague contextualization, too few documents, zero HIPP sourcing.

Students spent all their time on MCQ-format content review. You can't fix a writing problem with more multiple-choice practice.

## Key Insight

AP World is NOT AP HuGeo. HuGeo tests geographic concepts through maps/data. World tests historical reasoning through primary source documents. The HuGeo fix (better practice layer) was insufficient for World. World needs the practice layer AND explicit skill instruction (HIPP, thesis writing, contextualization, argument construction).
