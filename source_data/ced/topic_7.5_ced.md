# CED Data -- Topic 7.5: Unresolved Tensions After World War I

## Unit: 7 -- Global Conflict
## Exam Weight: 8-10%
## Time Period: c. 1900-present

## Thematic Focus
- Governance (GOV)

## Learning Objectives
- LO E: Explain the continuities and changes in territorial holdings from 1900 to the present.

## Essential Knowledge (Historical Developments)
- KC-6.2.I.B: Between the two world wars, Western and Japanese imperial states predominantly maintained control over colonial holdings; in some cases, they gained additional territories through conquest or treaty settlement and in othercasesfacedanti-imperialresistance.
