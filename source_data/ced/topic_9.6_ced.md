## Essential Knowledge (Historical Developments)
- KC-6.3.IV.i: Political and social changes of the 20th century led to changes in the arts and in the second half of the century, popular and consumer culture became more global.
- KC-6.3.IV.ii: Arts, entertainment, and popular culture increasinglyreflectedtheinfluenceofa globalizedsociety.
- KC-6.3.IV.iii: Consumerculturebecameglobalizedand transcended national borders.
