## Why These Courses Are Needed

**The writing crisis is real, but content gaps make it worse.** 60% of the AP World exam is writing (DBQ 25% + SAQ 20% + LEQ 15%), and our four at-risk students scored 1-2/7 on the DBQ with the same four gaps: no thesis, vague contextualization, too few documents, zero HIPP sourcing. But you can't write about Chinese industrialization if you don't know what happened — the content courses give students the historical knowledge they need to apply HIPP analysis.

**Units 3-6 (1450-1900) are ~50% of the exam.** Both China and Russia courses cover this high-weight period heavily. The 2023-2024 released exams focused on Chinese communism, Japanese imperialism, Qing decline, and Ottoman reforms — all topics in these courses.
